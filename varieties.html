🍫 Chocolate Varieties

Dark Chocolate

70% cocoa and above. Rich, intense, slightly bitter. Good for health in moderation.

Best for: Baking, eating plain
Milk Chocolate

Creamy, sweet, contains milk powder. Most popular for eating.

Best for: Candy bars, desserts
White Chocolate

Cocoa butter, sugar, milk solids. No cocoa solids. Buttery and sweet.

Best for: Desserts, pairing with berries
Ruby Chocolate

Naturally pink chocolate with berry fruity notes. Newest variety.

Best for: Special desserts
Couverture

High-quality chocolate with extra cocoa butter. Used by professionals.

Best for: Tempering, coating, baking
Unsweetened Chocolate

Pure chocolate liquor, no sugar. Very bitter.

Best for: Baking from scratch

🍚 Rice Varieties

Basmati Rice

Long-grain, aromatic, fluffy. Grown in Himalayas. Perfect for biryani and pilaf.

Best for: Indian dishes, pilaf
Jasmine Rice

Fragrant, slightly sticky. Thai cuisine staple.

Best for: Thai food, stir-fry
Arborio Rice

Short-grain, high starch, creamy. Used for risotto.

Best for: Risotto, rice pudding
Brown Rice

Whole grain with bran intact. Nutty flavor, chewy texture. Healthier option.

Best for: Healthy meals, bowls
Wild Rice

Not actually rice - aquatic grass seed. Chewy, nutty, black color.

Best for: Salads, stuffing
Sushi Rice

Short-grain, sticky when cooked. Seasoned with vinegar for sushi.

Best for: Sushi, onigiri
Black Rice

Also called forbidden rice. Deep purple when cooked. Nutty, rich in antioxidants.

Best for: Colorful bowls, desserts
Red Rice

Reddish bran, nutty flavor, chewy texture. Traditional in Bhutan and France.

Best for: Hearty meals

🥖 Bread Varieties

Sourdough

Naturally fermented with wild yeast. Tangy flavor, chewy texture.

Baguette

French bread, long and thin, crispy crust, soft inside.

Whole Wheat

Made with whole grain flour. Denser, more fiber.

Rye Bread

Dense, dark, earthy flavor. Common in Eastern Europe.

Ciabatta

Italian bread, flat and broad, holey texture. Great for sandwiches.

Focaccia

Italian flat bread, seasoned with olive oil and herbs.

🧀 Cheese Varieties

Cheddar

Sharp, tangy, aged. Most popular cheese.

Mozzarella

Fresh, mild, stretchy. Perfect for pizza.

Brie

Soft, creamy, edible white rind. French delicacy.

Parmesan

Hard, granular, nutty. Aged long time.

Blue Cheese

Sharp, salty, with blue veins from mold.

Goat Cheese

Tangy, soft, made from goat milk.
